About UTFC

  • The University of Toronto’s Finance Conference (UTFC), is Investment Society’s annual flagship event, composed of a case competition and networking session designed to empower, inspire and educate students about the finance industry. The conference invites industry professionals from many areas such as: investment banking, private equity, fixed income, mergers and acquisition, sales and training, and commodities to share their expertise with top talent across Ontario universities. Students will be competing in a finance case completion, and have the opportunity to build valuable relationships with industry professional during the networking session. This year, UTFC will be held at University of Toronto’s Hart House.

    For more information about investment society, visit our website: www.investmentsociety.ca

Mission statement

"To bridge the gap between the classroom and the finance industry by fostering practical learning opportunities for professional development and advancement. Through Investment Society’s unique competitions, insightful workshops, networking sessions and career trainings, students are equipped with necessary skills to gain a competitive edge in the dynamic world of finance. Our diverse team of 25 students work hard to ensure we deliver value to our members through carefully planned initiatives."

- Investment Society
